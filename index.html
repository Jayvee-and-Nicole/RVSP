Story

Our Love Story

Ahente nahulog sa Bumbero

(Ikaw san ka nahulog?)

Jayvee and Nicole first crossed paths as college classmates and became part of the same “barkada”. Their friendship was marked by shared adventures and a clever tactic to avoid long lines in the jeepney “ninja move is the key”. The playful banter from their friends soon turned into something deeper. As time went on, Jayvee had the courage to court Nicole. After a year of courtship, on December 3, 2013, Nicole gave Jayvee her sweetest yes. Their relationship was genuine and full of promise, but like many couples, they faced challenges. Unfortunately, after eight months together, Nicole decided to break up with Jayvee. Both went through the year-long separation without pursuing other relationships, and the teasing from friends continued, highlighting the undeniable feelings they still had for each other. Nicole took the first step by reaching out to Jayvee with a simple yet profound text Message: Do you still love me? Jayvee’s heartfelt response was a resounding yes. With renewed hope, Jayvee began courting Nicole again, and they rekindled their relationship on October 12, 2015. Their renewed commitment was a testament to their enduring love and support for one another. Together, they graduated in 2016 and passed the Criminologist Board Exam later that same year. Although their professional paths diverged, their unwavering support and love have remained constant. Their hearts always choose each other, proving that true love endures all trials.

Our Engament

February 3,2024

Since Jayvee and Nicole were together for 8 years all of their family, relatives and friends are all waiting for this moment to happen. Jayvee began planning his proposal in January 2024, working closely with Nicole’s sister (Ate Von) and her cousin (Jovikaye), to craft the perfect surprise. The plan centered around a simple yet meaningful dinner celebrating Nicole’s 28th birthday, along with the birthdays of Papa Resty (Nicole’s dad) and Jerwin (Jayvee’s brother).This thoughtful arrangement made the proposal even more special, blending celebration with romance. On February 3, 2024, during Nicole’s birthday celebration, Jayvee’s proposal unfolded in a beautiful and unexpected way. The romantic setup at Camp Benjamin in Alfonso, Cavite, created a perfect backdrop for this unforgettable moment. Jayvee’s proposal was a seamless blend of surprise and sentiment, capturing the essence of their love story. Nicole and Jayvee will always cherish this day as the beginning of their lifetime together, surrounded by the love and support of their closest family and friends.
